## Japan 2025 Tech Landscape Context

### The "2025 Digital Cliff" (2025年の崖)
Japan's Ministry of Economy, Trade and Industry (METI) warned that failure to modernize legacy systems could result in:
- **¥12 trillion** in annual economic losses by 2025
- **70% of IT systems** considered legacy by 2025
- Critical shortage of engineers familiar with COBOL, mainframe systems

### Key Market Statistics (2025)
| Metric | Value | Source |
|--------|-------|--------|
| IT Talent Shortage | 790,000 engineers by 2030 | METI |
| Cloud Adoption Growth | 45% planning increase | IDC Japan |
| Aging Population | 29.1% over 65 years | Ministry of Internal Affairs |
| Mobile Penetration | 91.4% smartphone usage | Ministry of Communications |
| Cashless Payment Growth | 40%+ adoption rate | Payments Japan |
| SME Digital Adoption | Only 30% digitized | SME Agency |

### Cultural & Business Considerations
- **Hanko (印鑑):** Traditional seal stamps still required for ~70% of business documents
- **Fax Usage:** 84% of companies still use fax machines
- **LINE Dominance:** 94 million monthly active users in Japan
- **Convenience Store Culture:** 56,000+ konbini serving as logistics/payment hubs
- **Cash Preference:** Despite growth, 60% still prefer cash transactions
- **Keigo (敬語):** Formal language requirements in business software
- **Nemawashi (根回し):** Consensus-building culture affects software adoption

## Development Guidelines

### Localization Requirements
1. **Language Support**
   - Japanese (primary)
   - English (secondary)
   - Support for vertical text (縦書き) where appropriate

2. **Date/Time Formats**
   - Japanese Era calendar support (令和)
   - 24-hour time format
   - JST timezone handling

3. **Number Formats**
   - Japanese numeral system (万、億)
   - Yen currency formatting

4. **Input Methods**
   - IME support for Japanese input
   - Furigana (reading aids) support
   - Half-width/full-width character handling

### Accessibility Standards
- WCAG 2.1 AA compliance
- Large text options for elderly users
- High contrast modes
- Screen reader compatibility
- Voice navigation support

### Security Requirements
- PDPA (Personal Data Protection Act) compliance
- APPI (Act on Protection of Personal Information) compliance
- Secure data handling for My Number system
- SSL/TLS encryption mandatory
